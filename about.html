Biography

Jason is a Computer Science major at Cornell University with minors in Applied Mathematics and Business. He has interned at Meta, HashiCorp, and Artisan Partners, where he contributed to large-scale backend systems, cloud authorization frameworks, and data processing pipelines. Jason is passionate about building scalable software, from gRPC-based microservices to AI-driven decision systems, with a focus on reliability and developer experience. Outside of school/work, he enjoys working out, playing and watching sports, playing poker, and traveling.
